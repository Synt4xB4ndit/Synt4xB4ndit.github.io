Humble Beginnings

I remember being new to Solana NFTs in October, 2021. I had gotten rugged and threw money at projects with no intentions of building. Eventually in one of those projects I met a kid named Cutthroat. He had plans to create a DAO on Solana to help new people come together and learn to get better and share 'alpha'. Almost a year later it's grown to be so much more than that. I best describe it at a tight-knit group of dedicated men & women full of respect, yet degeneracy.

About PrimusDAO

PrimusDAO runs Daily Twitter spaces that yield 70-150 spectators. During the Bull market we like to run Mints of the Day. Things have been rather Bear, therefore we have focussed on secondary plays, good vibes and making the best of an otherwise rough market. Within PrimusDAO we have a Board of Directors, or the Senate. These members bring trusted, skilled, reliable, yet diverse source of information. We have an investment fund in which we vote on our plays in the Senate. We encourage ALL Primus members to be active as genuinely people love Primus. Recently, we had just help a Poker Tourny and a lot of members had a blast. If you're interested in joining we send out Invite Links often via PrimusDAO Twitter offical account, you can find here. Otherwise you could reach out the Cutthroat, Wes, or Joey. Probably not Joey.
