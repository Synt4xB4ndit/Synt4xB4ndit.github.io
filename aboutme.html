About Me

It's been a journey...

I remember being 5 years old. The endless possibilities of who I'd be through experiences. They always asked me what I wanted to do when I grew up. All I knew is I LOVED playing Pokemon. My friends and I had plans to create our own gamestop-esq type video game store as well as create the games for them! Turns out that didn't end up happening... Nonetheless, I graduated a Vocational Highschool in the Welding trade. I have also done human services and ABA work for 10 years through highschool and college. I never felt I had my heart or passion in any of these jobs though I enjoyed giving back and the relationships I had made. However, it wasn't something I'd wanted to do forever. Putting my childhood dream together with my investing knowledge & NFT trading it was inevitable that I'd begin to learn Software Development. My goals and intentions are to learn programming languages to create projects and add value. I also want to learn cyber-security and how to build a computer one day. Thanks for learning a little about myself and I hope it inspires you to pursue your dreams . Feel free to reach out for scheduling. Blessings
